sanctuary.


Since 2010, the government in Canmore Alberta has spent over four hundred thousand dollars euthanizing european domestic rabbits. However, as of September 2020 there are still thousands living in the Canmore area. Therefore, these attempts have been mostly unsuccessful in creating a compassionate, sustainable and effective solution to the problem.

We propose the contruction of a large scale sanctuary facility as a proof of concept to sustainably house 200 domestic rabbits.

This Facility this will be heated with GPU cryptocurrency mining hardware, generating a net profit while providing perfect weather coniditions inside. Other sources of monitization will include indoor vertical farming, tourism, and academic study.

Not only will this building pay for itself, but it will preserve the lives of hudreds of animals. This relieves local governments from hundreds of thousands of tax dollars while simultaneously stimulating the local economy by creating new jobs, and standing as a halmark of canadian innovation, sustainbility, and compassion.
