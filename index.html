our vision.


Our vision is to save abandoned and feral rabbits, at scale. European domestic rabbits have been promoted and sold as house pets for the last several decades. Increased popularity has naturally led to increased abandonment, causing alarming and destructive population growths.

Many of these animals suffer a long and painful death because their ability to defend from predators and survive extreme temperatures is very limited. They can also cause hundreds of thousands of dollars in landscaping damage, and disrupt the delicate balance of the natural ecologies they invade.

At Mythica, we strive to end suffering for animals that are lost, forgotten and abused. In doing so, we simultaneously offer civic relief to governments and communities. First by redirecting tax dollars to other issues and second by helping to prevent the destruction of residential and commercial landscapes, as well as local ecologies.
